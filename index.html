Herbi

Herbivores are animals whose primary food source is plant- based. Examples of herbivores include vertebrates like deer, koalas, and some ird species.

Carni

Carnivores are animals that eat other animals. The word carnivore is derived from Latin and literally means “meat eater.” Wild cats such as lions etc

Omni

Omnivores are animals that eat both plant- and animal-derived food. In Latin, omnivore means to eat everything. Humans and bears.
